Privacy Policy

This app, Call Monitor & Auto SMS, does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Permissions Used:

  • READ_CALL_LOG: Used to detect the latest call (missed, incoming, or outgoing).
  • READ_PHONE_STATE: Used to monitor call state transitions.
  • SEND_SMS: Used to send auto SMS based on user-configured settings.

All functionality is performed locally on your device. We do not collect or transmit any user data to our servers or third parties.

No data is used for analytics or advertisements.
